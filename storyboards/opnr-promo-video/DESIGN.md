# opnr.gg Promo Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark tactical desktop control room for TFT decision-making. The video should feel like a premium game HUD and a data terminal, not a SaaS landing page. Use sharp panels, dot-matrix field texture, high-contrast Rajdhani type, gold source/evidence signal, acid active states, and violet depth glow.

## Colors

- Background: `#03050c`
- Deep panel: `#050812`
- Panel glass: `rgba(7, 11, 21, 0.88)`
- Text: `#f8fbff`
- Soft text: `#c9d1de`
- Gold signal: `#f0b347`
- Acid active: `#d8ff00`
- Violet depth: `#8560df`

## Typography

- Primary: Rajdhani Bold/SemiBold
- Labels: Rajdhani SemiBold uppercase
- Use large condensed uppercase headlines for the promo message.
- Use monospaced-feeling tight labels only for HUD metadata.

## Motion

- Hard data snaps for selected states.
- Fast row and panel entrances, then readable holds.
- Gold scan lines should feel like source validation.
- Violet depth motion should remain behind the product UI.
- Avoid soft SaaS bounces, rounded marketing cards, and generic gradient text.

## What Not To Do

- Do not make it look like op.gg or Mobalytics.
- Do not hide the app behind abstract glow.
- Do not overuse blur or glitch on readable UI.
- Do not turn the ending into a generic app-store CTA.
- Do not use cyan/purple neon as the dominant palette.
