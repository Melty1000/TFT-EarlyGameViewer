Final proposition

A tactical product film, not a generic gaming ad.

The video should make opnr.gg feel like an expert table built into the desktop: fast, dense, source-aware, and playable under pressure.

Creative direction

Dark dot-matrix workspace, sharp Rajdhani typography, gold decision signal, violet depth, acid-green active state, minimal rounded corners, readable UI.

Must-show product moments

  • Build browser with source, rank, phase, evidence, champions, augments, and components.
  • Selected build populating board view, overview, synergies, items, leveling, and augments.
  • Similarity filtering by champions, items, traits, and current board signals.
  • Desktop-first draggable panel layout as the signature UI identity.

Production next step

Turn this storyboard into an animatic: export 4K UI plates, record cursor/panel motion passes, cut to a 45-second timing track, then animate typography, camera, parallax, and signal lines in After Effects or HyperFrames.
